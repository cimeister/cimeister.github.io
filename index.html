PhD Student in Computer Science

ETH Zürich

I’m a second year PhD in Computer Science with Professor Ryan Cotterell at ETH Zürich. I am passionate about the general applications of statistics and information theory to natural language processing; lately, my research has been on decoding methods for sequence models. In my free time, I go rock climbing, trail running, and just about everything that falls in between the two. You can check out some of my adventures on my husband’s blog: http://www.timaiken.org/blog
